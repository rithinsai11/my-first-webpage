About

Rithin’s serves spirited, Italian-influenced food that reflects a longstanding commitment to local agriculture and sustainability. Our menu celebrates our relationships with area farmers, tradition-based artisans, agricultural-based Italian importers, and winemakers. While the experience at Rithin’s showcases our focus on procuring the highest quality food and wine, we are, above all, committed to making our industry a more equitable and sustainable home for all of those who work in it.
